About

During the war, in addition to entertainment purposes, manga was also used for propaganda or satirical purposes to serve the national interest, so during this time Japanese comics were subjected to heavy censorship and censorship. The development of Japanese manga seems to have been postponed indefinitely

After the war, ASAMU TEZUKA stood up to revive the manga industry, bringing to Japanese culture and the world a whole new genre of manga. He helped shape the first true manga model and started an industry that still holds a strategic place in modern Japanese culture.

Oda Eiichiro

The author of the manga one piece

Oda Eiichirō is a Japanese manga artist, currently writing for Shūeisha Publishing House. He is the author of the world famous series One Piece.

Kishimoto Masashi

The author of the manga Naruto

Kishimoto Masashi born on November 8, 1974 in Okayama is a manga artist who is known for the world famous manga series Naruto.

Tam Thiếu

Duong Gia Tam Thieu, real name is Truong Uy. Born on January 10, 1981 in Beijing, is a Chinese internet writer, chairman of Tangmen Illusory Culture Co., Ltd., graduated from Hebei Law Institute.

Toriyama Akira

The author of the manga Dragon ball

Toriyama Akira was born on April 5, 1955 in Nagoya, Aichi, Japan. He is a Japanese manga author, best known for Dragon Ball and Dr. Slump.
